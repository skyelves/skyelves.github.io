Ke Wang

Ke Wang is currently a CS PhD student at Yale.

Ke reveiced a Bachelor of Science degree in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science with a minor in Economics in the National School of Development at Peking University in China in 2020, where he conducts researches on sketches under the advice of Prof. Tong Yang. He also conducts researches on Machine Learning for security under the advise of Prof. Xin Jin. After graduating from PKU, he spent one year as a post-undergraduate in the Shanghai Qi Zhi Institute advised by Prof. Mingyu Gao working on Non-Volatile Memory.

Ke's research interests lie in the fields of computer system, network and database, including efficient memory architectures, network telemetry and programmable network and database engine.
